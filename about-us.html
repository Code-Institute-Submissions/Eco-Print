About Us


Industry leaders in Eco printing technology. We are reducing paper and card waste by recycling your old material and creating fanastic printable products including leaflets and business cards for example.


Worldwide, 4 billion trees are cut down every year for paper. That’s one per cent of the Amazon Rainforest every single year. A consequence of this is animals and fauna dying and losing their homes in return destroying our eco system.


There are numerous environmental consequences of not recycling paper. Paper is a renewable resource but the rate of use exceeds the rate of replacement for the plants and trees harvested. A result of this is a reduction in available resources which has an impact on several different areas of the environment. Recycling is one necessary step towards conserving the valuable plant and forest resources. If you would like to read more about this and the effects of not recycling paper Click Here.


Our mission is to do all that we can to reduce our carbon footprint and clean the planet up one leaflet at a time.


Our products are 100% Recycled paper and Hemp products, this means you can contribute to saving our planet when you purchase from us!

We will also collect and recycle your left over leaflets free of charge and for every order of over £100 we will plant a new sapling to create a better future.


In addition to this we are also conducting kerb-side paper and card board collection and we pledge to recycle 100% of all paper waste we collect.
